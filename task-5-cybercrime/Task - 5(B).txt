Task – 5: Part –B Stay Safe Online.      

Prevention checklist for college students in India
 

Use unique passwords for your email, social media and academic accounts. 

Do not use the password for all of your accounts, this is not safe. 

Enable two-factor authentication whenever you can this will add a layer of security to your online accounts. 

Try to avoid using Wi-Fi for things like banking or logging into your academic portal it is just not worth the risk. 

Never click on links or open attachments from people you do not know this can be very dangerous. 

Always check first before you open anything. 

Keep your devices updated with the software and antivirus protection; this will help prevent security problems. 

Be careful when you share information online like your phone number or address College Students in India need to be careful, with this.
