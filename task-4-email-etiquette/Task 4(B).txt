Part B – Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist: 

 

Checklist for Students Regarding Social Media – Dos and Don’ts 


DO's: 

Think Before You Post. Your Posts Should Reflect Your Values and Be Respectful. 

Make Sure Your Social Media Profile Is Professional and Up To Date. (Clear Profile Picture/Bio) 

Protect Your Privacy. Make Sure You Check Appropriate Settings on Social Media Sites. 

Verify That the Information You Share Is Accurate. Avoid Spreading False Information Online. 

Keep Discussions/Interactions Online Positive and Respectful by Being Courteous & Supporting People Who Are Posting Online. 

  

DONT's: 

Share Your Personal/Sensitive Information Online (Phone Numbers, Addresses, Passwords, etc....) 

Post Inappropriate/Offensive Material on Social Media That Might Impact Future Employment & Educational Opportunities. 

Engage In Arguments Online. Stay Calm and Do Not Create Unneeded Conflict Online. 

Do Not Overshare Personal Activities. This May Jeopardize Your Safety And Privacy. 

Do Not CLICK On Any Suspicious Links; They Could Be scams/harmful.
